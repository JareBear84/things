The performance art where every line and scene is created on the spot. The common phrase taught to improvisors is "yes, and..." in order to emphasis the importance of not destroying the reality that is being established, but instead help build and add to the scenes, relationships and environment.

But... Why?

It. Is. Difficult.Not only do imporvisors need to make strong emotional, relational or environmental choices instantly, or remember newly created objects, names, and other details as they happen, but improvisors have to communicate with each otehr as characters and on the meta level to ensure they are on the same page. It is that trust building and willingness to be vulnerable that makes it worthwhile when a good scene or game comes together.
