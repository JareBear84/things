How Do You Create Such Mediocrity?

Being a husky guy, I've been compared to a bear my whole life. Even to the point I was nicknamed "Jarebear" during my undergrad yearsI use a common technique called wet-on-wet. It's where the canvas is pre-painted with a base coat of paint thet helps the paint blend and push easier. Bob Ross, the famous painter of the 80s and 90s used the same style.

Easy to learn, but difficult to master. It's messy, smelly, and frustrating, butI love it!
